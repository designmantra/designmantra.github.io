DESIGNER


Geetha Vasishta brings a unique international perspective to her position as a full service interior designer who works with prominent residential and commercial clients throughout the South Bay area.


She has a Masters Degree in Liberal Arts and Diploma in Interior Design from India. As the region’s only design professional trained in India, Geetha provides a novel level of sophistication and style to her projects in Los Angeles County, including Manhattan Beach, Redondo Beach, Torrance, and Palos Verdes; Orange County, and other communities.

She offers residential design services ranging from project management and space planning to furniture selection and remodeling upgrades for business owners, entrepreneurs, top executives, other professionals, and homemakers. She also designs the interiors of retail stores, restaurants, and other commercial establishments.

Since she’s skilled in both residential and commercial design, Geetha can assist clients with both their living and work space. She works on both new and existing homes.

Geetha, who specializes in color, space planning and furniture placement, is also a gifted interior consultant. She is known as a “problem solver” who advises clients on such topics as organizing space, maximizing storage, selecting paint, positioning furnishings and designing closets.

Geetha also draws interior design inspiration from vastu shastra, Indian architectural science similar to the Chinese feng shui, and incorporates serenity and balance into her work. Clients rave about the sense of peace and harmony she introduces in their homes, condos, apartments, offices, shops and restaurants.

Her company, Design Mantra, works with the area’s foremost vendors and suppliers of fine furnishings. The firm also has the flexibility to work with its clients’ contractors.

Geetha’s clients benefit from the customer service, product knowledge and attention to detail she gained during her highly successful career working in her business, as a freelancer and in retail store design departments in India and United States.

An avid volunteer, Geetha has supported her community over the years which includes, leading the Parent Teacher Association (PTA) program chairs. She served as a judge at the Annual Children Drawing Contest conducted by South Bay Taiwanese Mothers Association (TMA) of Southern California. She is currently one of the Board of Directors of South Bay Conservatory, a non-profit performing arts school, governed by all-volunteers in the panel.

FAQ's


How do you charge your design fees?

Except for the Design Consultation Service, which is a flat fee, the fees for the rest of our design services are based on scope of work.


Your Design Consultation Service is for 2 hours. What if I need more consulting time?

We have a solution for that. You are welcome to purchase a block of extra consulting time of 2 to 4 hours at a reduced rate.


Once I have paid the initial consultation, can I upgrade to another design service?

Yes, absolutely! Please let your Designer know within 30 days of your paid initial consultation that you want to upgrade the design service. The investment in the initial consultation will be credited into your Letter of Agreement if you decide to do your project with Design Mantra.


Does your design service include purchasing fee?

The design fee does not include purchasing fee, it is for professional design service only. Merchandise purchasing services will be provided by Design Mantra after design work has been completed, approved, signed and payment is made prior to ordering. Purchase of merchandise will be provided to you at Design Mantra's most favorable price plus purchasing fee which includes administrative and operational expenses to handle details pertaining to the process.


What about accessories? Are they included in your design implementation?

The accessories are not included in our design fees. Design implementation does not cover selection of accessories other than during normal shopping for general specification purposes.


Can we contact you during weekends?

Design Mantra business hours are 9:30 AM to 4:30 PM PT, Monday through Friday. If you are calling after hours or on weekends, please note that you can expect to receive a response the following business day.
